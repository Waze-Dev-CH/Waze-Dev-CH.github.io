# Difficult turns

The "difficult turns" feature lets you flag a complicated movement at an intersection, so that Waze can avoid it according to the driver's preferences.

## What it is for

This feature is meant for intersections without traffic lights where turning right is simple, but where crossing or turning left can be very difficult to perform because of priority traffic. The movement remains allowed, but Waze penalises it according to the defined settings (permanently or by day / time ranges).

On the driver's side, the preference is set in the app under Settings, then Navigation, to indicate whether they want to avoid this kind of turn.

## How to use it in WME

It is up to the local community to flag difficult turns, via a checkbox in the editor. Only editors of level 2 and above can change this feature.

::: failure Error
- Do not use this feature at intersections equipped with traffic lights.
- Do not use it for personal purposes to negatively influence a route.
:::

::: help When in doubt
When in doubt, open a discussion on the forum before marking a turn as difficult.
:::
