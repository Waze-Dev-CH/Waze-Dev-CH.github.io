# Lanes and railroad crossings

Lane guidance and railroad crossings follow editing rules specific to Switzerland.

::: important Reserved for L3 editors and above
Configuring lane guidance is reserved for editors of level L3 and above. Railroad crossings require an even higher level (L4 and above). If you do not have the required level, do not edit these objects.
:::

## Lane guidance (lanes)

Lane guidance tells the driver which lane or lanes to use when approaching an intersection. Its configuration in Switzerland follows dedicated guidelines.

## Railroad crossings

::: note Note
Since June 2024, the rules relating to railroad crossings have been moved into Waze's "Permanent Hazards" guidelines.
:::
