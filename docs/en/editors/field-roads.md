# Field roads (Feldwege)

Field roads (Feldwege, agricultural roads) follow a unified agreement for Switzerland. The classification depends on two criteria: the existence of a driving ban and the state of the surface.

## Principle

Waze is a community traffic app, not an off-road navigation tool. The classification favours the needs of ordinary traffic rather than specialised uses.

## Classification

### Dirt road with driving ban

For unpaved paths closed to traffic (except residents or authorised vehicles):

- segment type: Private Road
- effect: blocks all traffic, except to reach a destination located on this segment

### Dirt road without driving ban

Two options depending on the terrain:

- Street with the Unpaved attribute: adds a penalty that the user can adjust in their preferences (allow, avoid, avoid long stretches). Recommended when the choice can be left to the user.
- Off-road / Not Maintained: applies a very strong penalty, routing is blocked in almost all cases. Reserved for genuinely impassable terrain (true 4x4 tracks).

### Paved country road without driving ban

Two options:

- Street type
- Narrow Road type, preferred for single-lane passages

## About the Unpaved attribute

::: note Note
The Unpaved attribute can be added to any Street and adds a penalty that the user can choose to avoid or not. Applied to a segment that already carries a type penalty (Private Road, Off-road), it has no effect.
:::
